# DAYY Reference Comparison

## Source

- Reference: `https://www.dayy.com/en`
- Extracted title: `DAYY® – Let's rethink tomorrow.`
- Assets captured in `design-extractor/screenshots/`.
- Raw computed tokens saved in `design-extractor/raw.json`.

## Extracted Patterns

1. Full-viewport atmospheric hero with dark image treatment and a small brand lockup.
2. Bottom floating pill navigation with low visual weight.
3. Oversized editorial typography for the core proposition.
4. White space first, cards second; repeated tiles use pale gray surfaces and tight gaps.
5. Deep gradient service sections with white stacked panels.
6. Footer uses sparse metadata and one oversized brand line.

## Applied To 即宿科技

1. Hero rebuilt around "今晚订房 / 先看尾房好价" with dark hotel imagery and amber night-light accent.
2. Primary navigation moved to a bottom floating pill: 用户、规则、酒店、联系.
3. Dense product explanation collapsed into large statement blocks and four key signals.
4. Rules and hotel cooperation sections use DAYY-like large left headline plus stacked panels.
5. Company and contact data are intentionally sparse and operationally credible.
6. Real information updated: mini program code 暂无, ICP 暂无, contact email `jisugroup@126.com`, cooperation form 暂无.

## Intentional Differences

1. Copy is clearer and more transactional than DAYY because this is a C端 local-life service, not a pure brand studio page.
2. Mobile signal cards use a 2x2 grid instead of horizontal overflow, improving readability for ordinary booking users.
3. Chinese typography keeps `letter-spacing: 0` to avoid cramped mobile rendering.
4. Gradients are anchored in hotel-night amber and deep teal rather than abstract brand color only.
